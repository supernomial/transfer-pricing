Solara Distribution France SAS was incorporated in 2012 as the group's primary distribution arm for the French market. The entity is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Solara Group BV (Netherlands) and operates from its headquarters in Paris with a distribution center near Lyon.

The entity's strategy focuses on maintaining market share in established retail channels while expanding its e-commerce presence. During FY2024, online sales represented approximately 28% of total revenue, up from 22% in the prior year. Management targets 35% e-commerce penetration by FY2026.

Solara Distribution France employed 187 full-time equivalents as of December 31, 2024, and reported revenues of EUR 142 million for the fiscal year.
