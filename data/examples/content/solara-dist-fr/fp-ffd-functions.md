Solara Distribution France performs the following key functions:

- **Purchasing and procurement**: Independently negotiates purchase volumes and delivery schedules with Solara Group manufacturing entities and select third-party suppliers.
- **Warehousing and logistics**: Operates a 12,000 m2 distribution center near Lyon, managing inbound receipt, storage, picking, packing, and outbound delivery across France.
- **Sales and marketing**: Maintains a 45-person sales team covering national accounts, regional retailers, and e-commerce channels. Designs and executes local marketing campaigns within group brand guidelines.
- **Pricing**: Sets end-customer pricing autonomously, subject to group-wide floor pricing for brand protection.
- **After-sales and warranty**: Manages product returns, warranty claims, and customer support through a dedicated service center.
- **Credit management**: Extends trade credit to customers and manages receivables collection independently.
