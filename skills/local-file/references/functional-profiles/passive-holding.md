# Passive Holding

Slug: `passive-holding`

## Profile overview
Holds shares in subsidiaries with minimal active management. Exists primarily for legal, tax, or structural reasons. Does not perform strategic or operational functions.

## Functions — topics to cover
- **Shareholding Administration**: How share registers, ownership records, and corporate filings are maintained.
- **Dividend Collection & Distribution**: How dividends from subsidiaries are received and passed up or retained.
- **Board Representation (minimal, non-executive)**: Nature and scope of any board seats or oversight involvement.
- **Statutory Compliance & Reporting**: Minimum legal and regulatory obligations to remain in good standing.
- **Minimal Corporate Governance**: Basic corporate formalities without substantive decision-making.

## Assets — topics to cover
- **Shareholdings in Subsidiaries**: The equity investments held and their structure.
- **Minimal Cash & Bank Balances**: Cash needed for basic corporate operations and dividend flows.
- **Minimal Office Space/Infrastructure**: Any dedicated premises or shared services used.

## Risks — topics to cover
- **Investment Risk (value of shareholdings)**: Exposure to subsidiary valuation changes.
- **Dividend Risk (dependent on subsidiary performance)**: Reliance on subsidiary profits for distributions.
- **Limited Regulatory Risk**: Compliance burden limited to holding-company obligations.
- **No Operational Risk**: No active business operations to create operational risk.

## Typical transaction types
- Rarely involved in intercompany transactions — primarily equity/dividend flows
