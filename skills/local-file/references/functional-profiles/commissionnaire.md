# Commissionnaire

Slug: `commissionnaire`

## Profile overview
Sells in own name but for the principal's account. Never owns the goods — title passes directly from principal to customer. Earns a commission.

## Functions — topics to cover
- **Sales Negotiation (in own name, for principal's account)**: Negotiate and contract with customers in own name, on principal’s behalf.
- **Customer Acquisition & Relationship Management**: Acquire and maintain customer relationships.
- **Market Intelligence & Reporting**: Gather and report market and customer intelligence.
- **Contract Administration**: Administer contracts between principal and customers.
- **Limited Local Marketing**: Carry out limited local marketing as directed.
- **Order Facilitation**: Facilitate orders and coordination between principal and customers.

## Assets — topics to cover
- **Minimal — Customer Relationships**: Relationships and contracts in commissionnaire’s name.
- **Market Knowledge**: Local market and customer knowledge.
- **Office Equipment**: Basic office equipment.
- **IT Systems (no inventory, no warehouse)**: Systems for orders and reporting; no inventory or warehouse.

## Risks — topics to cover
- **Very limited — No Inventory Risk (title stays with principal)**: Never holds title to goods.
- **Limited Credit Risk (depending on structure)**: Credit risk varies by arrangement.
- **No Price Risk on goods**: Principal bears price and margin risk on goods.
- **Commission at risk only**: Commission or fee at risk based on performance.

## Typical transaction types
- Services (commission/agency services)
- Tangible goods (facilitation only)
