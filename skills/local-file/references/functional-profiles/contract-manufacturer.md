# Contract Manufacturer

Slug: `contract-manufacturer`

## Profile overview

Manufactures products to the principal's specifications. Limited autonomy — does not own IP or bear market risk.

## Functions — topics to cover

- **Manufacturing per Specifications**: Production to principal's design and specs
- **Quality Control & Testing**: Conformance to principal's quality standards
- **Procurement (may be directed by principal)**: Sourcing scope and authority
- **Packaging & Labeling**: Packaging and labeling responsibilities
- **Production Scheduling**: Capacity planning and production coordination
- **Basic Logistics (shipping to principal)**: Delivery to principal or designated point

## Assets — topics to cover

- **Manufacturing Facilities (may be principal-owned)**: Facility ownership and control
- **Machinery & Equipment**: Production equipment ownership
- **Limited Inventory (may be consignment)**: Inventory scope and ownership
- **Basic Process Know-how**: Operational knowledge (no IP ownership)
- **IT Systems**: Systems for production and coordination

## Risks — topics to cover

- **Limited Market Risk (guaranteed off-take)**: Commitment from principal on volume
- **Limited Inventory Risk (buy-back clauses typical)**: Principal's inventory responsibility
- **Operational & Production Risk**: Production and delivery performance
- **Capacity Utilization Risk**: Idle capacity when volumes decline
- **Limited Credit Risk**: Payment terms and guarantees from principal

## Typical transaction types

- Tangible goods (as seller to principal)
- Services (manufacturing services)
