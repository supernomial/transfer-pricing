# Procurement Hub

Slug: `procurement-hub`

## Profile overview
Centralizes purchasing for the group — negotiates with suppliers, consolidates volumes, manages vendor relationships, and distributes goods/materials to group entities.

## Functions — topics to cover
- **Supplier Negotiation & Relationship Management**: How suppliers are selected, negotiated with, and managed over time.
- **Volume Consolidation & Purchasing**: How group demand is aggregated and purchase orders placed.
- **Quality Assurance & Vendor Auditing**: How supplier quality is assured and audited.
- **Inventory Management & Logistics**: How stock levels, warehousing, and distribution are coordinated.
- **Contract Management**: How supplier contracts are drafted, monitored, and renewed.
- **Market Intelligence & Price Monitoring**: How market prices and trends are tracked for sourcing decisions.
- **Compliance & Sustainability Standards**: How procurement meets group and regulatory standards.

## Assets — topics to cover
- **Supplier Contracts & Relationships**: Long-term contracts and strategic supplier ties.
- **Inventory (central stock)**: Goods held in centralized warehouses or transit.
- **Warehouse & Logistics Facilities**: Physical infrastructure for storage and distribution.
- **Procurement IT Systems**: Systems for sourcing, ordering, and supplier management.
- **Skilled Procurement Workforce**: People performing sourcing, negotiation, and logistics.
- **Market Intelligence & Data**: Information on prices, suppliers, and market conditions.

## Risks — topics to cover
- **Supply Chain & Disruption Risk**: Exposure to supply failures, logistics delays, or geopolitical disruption.
- **Price & Commodity Risk**: Exposure to raw material and input price volatility.
- **Inventory Risk (if holding stock)**: Obsolescence, damage, or writedowns on held stock.
- **Supplier Concentration Risk**: Dependence on few suppliers for critical inputs.
- **Quality Risk**: Defective or non-compliant goods from suppliers.
- **Limited Credit Risk**: Minimal credit exposure given typical payment terms.
- **FX Risk (international sourcing)**: Currency exposure when sourcing across borders.

## Typical transaction types
- Tangible goods (as buyer from external, seller to group)
- Services (procurement services)
