# Full-fledged Distributor

Slug: `full-fledged-distributor`

## Profile overview
Purchases goods for own account, owns inventory, and bears market risk. Makes pricing, marketing, and strategic distribution decisions independently.

## Functions — topics to cover
- **Market Development & Strategy**: Define and execute distribution strategy for the territory.
- **Sales & Customer Relationship Management**: Build and maintain direct customer relationships.
- **Pricing Decisions**: Set resale prices and negotiate terms with customers.
- **Inventory Management & Warehousing**: Hold and manage inventory for own account.
- **Marketing & Advertising**: Develop and run local marketing campaigns.
- **After-sales Service & Returns**: Handle warranties, returns, and post-sale support.
- **Distribution & Logistics**: Manage warehousing, delivery, and supply chain.
- **Credit Management & Collections**: Bear credit risk and collect receivables.
- **Administrative & Financial Management**: Handle local administration and finance.

## Assets — topics to cover
- **Inventory (own account, significant)**: Stock held for resale, not consignment.
- **Trade Receivables**: Customer receivables on distributor’s balance sheet.
- **Distribution Network & Warehouses**: Own or long-term leased facilities.
- **Customer Lists & Relationships**: Own customer base and relationships.
- **Marketing Intangibles (local brand building)**: Local brand and goodwill.
- **Vehicles & Transportation Fleet**: Own or controlled logistics assets.
- **IT Systems (ERP, CRM)**: Systems for sales, inventory, and operations.
- **Office & Warehouse Facilities**: Fixed assets for operations.

## Risks — topics to cover
- **Market & Demand Risk (full)**: Bear full exposure to demand and market conditions.
- **Inventory & Obsolescence Risk (significant)**: Bear risk of unsold or obsolete stock.
- **Credit & Collection Risk**: Bear risk of customer non-payment.
- **Price & Margin Risk**: Exposure to price movements and margin pressure.
- **Foreign Exchange Risk**: Currency exposure on purchases and sales.
- **Warranty & Returns Risk**: Bear cost of returns and warranty claims.
- **Competitive Risk**: Exposure to local competition and market share loss.

## Typical transaction types
- Tangible goods (as buyer/reseller)
- Licensing of intangibles (as licensee for marketing IP)
