# Limited-risk Distributor

Slug: `limited-risk-distributor`

## Profile overview
Distributes products with limited risk exposure. Typically guaranteed a routine return by the principal. Does not bear significant inventory, market, or credit risk.

## Functions — topics to cover
- **Order Processing & Fulfillment**: Process orders and coordinate delivery per principal’s terms.
- **Limited Marketing (executes principal's strategy)**: Implement marketing set by principal.
- **Inventory Management (limited — return/buy-back clauses)**: Manage stock subject to return rights.
- **Customer Interface & Sales Support**: Serve as local interface with customers.
- **Local Logistics Coordination**: Coordinate local delivery and warehousing.
- **Basic Administrative Functions**: Handle routine local administration.
- **Market Feedback to Principal**: Report market conditions and feedback to principal.

## Assets — topics to cover
- **Limited Inventory (return rights)**: Inventory held with buy-back or return clauses.
- **Trade Receivables**: Receivables, often with limited credit risk.
- **Minimal Fixed Assets**: Limited capital tied up in assets.
- **IT Systems**: Systems for orders, logistics, and reporting.
- **Office Space**: Basic office for local operations.

## Risks — topics to cover
- **Limited Market Risk (routine return guaranteed)**: Principal absorbs most demand risk.
- **Limited Inventory Risk (return/buy-back clauses)**: Can return or sell back unsold stock.
- **Limited Credit Risk**: Principal often retains or shares credit exposure.
- **Minimal Foreign Exchange Risk**: Limited FX exposure due to structured pricing.
- **Operational Risk only**: Mainly operational and execution risk.

## Typical transaction types
- Tangible goods (as buyer from principal)
- Services (distribution services)
