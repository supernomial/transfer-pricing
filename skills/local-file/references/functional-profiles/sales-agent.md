# Sales Agent / Representative

Slug: `sales-agent`

## Profile overview
Facilitates sales on behalf of the principal but does NOT contract in own name. Purely an intermediary — introduces customers, provides market intelligence. Even less risk than a commissionnaire.

## Functions — topics to cover
- **Customer Introduction & Prospecting**: Identify and introduce potential customers to the principal.
- **Market Information & Intelligence**: Gather and report market and competitive intelligence.
- **Sales Facilitation (no contracting authority)**: Support sales process without contracting authority.
- **Relationship Maintenance**: Maintain relationships with existing customers.
- **Trade Show & Event Support**: Represent principal at trade shows and events.
- **Local Market Reporting**: Report on local market conditions and opportunities.

## Assets — topics to cover
- **Customer Contact Network**: Network of contacts and introductions.
- **Market Knowledge**: Local market and industry knowledge.
- **Minimal Office Equipment**: Basic equipment for communication and administration.

## Risks — topics to cover
- **Very limited — No Inventory Risk**: Never holds or owns goods.
- **No Credit Risk**: No exposure to customer credit.
- **No Contracting Risk**: Does not contract in own name.
- **Commission/fee at risk only**: Only commission or fee is at risk.

## Typical transaction types
- Services (agency/representation services)
