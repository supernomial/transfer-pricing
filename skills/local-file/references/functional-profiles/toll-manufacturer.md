# Toll Manufacturer

Slug: `toll-manufacturer`

## Profile overview

Performs processing/conversion only using principal's raw materials. Most limited manufacturing role — essentially a "fee-for-service" converter.

## Functions — topics to cover

- **Processing & Conversion only**: Transformation of principal's materials; no design or specification ownership
- **Quality Control of conversion process**: Ensuring conversion meets agreed specs
- **Material Handling (of principal's materials)**: Receipt, storage, and handling of principal's inputs/outputs
- **Minimal administrative functions**: Basic coordination and documentation

## Assets — topics to cover

- **Processing Equipment (may be principal-owned)**: Equipment ownership and control
- **No inventory ownership**: Materials remain principal's throughout
- **Limited process know-how**: Operational conversion capability only
- **Basic facilities**: Processing site (may be principal-owned or leased)

## Risks — topics to cover

- **Operational Risk only**: Conversion quality, timing, and capacity
- **No market risk**: Principal bears all market and demand risk
- **No inventory risk**: No ownership of raw materials or finished goods
- **No credit risk (typically pre-funded)**: Principal typically funds materials and conversion
- **Capacity utilization risk**: Idle capacity when principal's volumes decline

## Typical transaction types

- Services (toll/conversion services)
- Tangible goods (converted products returned to principal)
