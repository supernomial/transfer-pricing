# Full-fledged Manufacturer

Slug: `full-fledged-manufacturer`

## Profile overview

Manufactures products using own IP, R&D, and strategic decisions. Bears full entrepreneurial risk.

## Functions — topics to cover

- **Manufacturing & Processing**: Production activities, scale, and capabilities
- **Research & Development**: In-house R&D activities and innovation role
- **Quality Control**: Quality management and product certification
- **Procurement & Supply Chain**: Sourcing decisions and supplier relationships
- **Product Design & Engineering**: Design ownership and technical development
- **Marketing & Sales Strategy**: Market positioning and sales approach
- **Distribution & Logistics**: Warehousing, shipping, and delivery
- **After-sales Service & Warranty**: Customer support, repairs, and warranty obligations
- **Administrative & Financial Management**: Back-office and financial controls

## Assets — topics to cover

- **Manufacturing Facilities & Property**: Owned or leased production sites
- **Machinery & Equipment**: Production capital and capacity
- **Raw Material & WIP Inventory**: Inventory ownership and management
- **Process IP & Trade Secrets**: Proprietary processes and know-how
- **Patents & Technical Know-how**: IP ownership and technical assets
- **IT Systems & ERP**: Systems for planning, production, and finance
- **Quality Management Systems**: Certification and quality infrastructure
- **R&D Facilities**: Laboratories and development infrastructure
- **Workforce (technical & managerial)**: Technical staff and management capacity

## Risks — topics to cover

- **Market & Demand Risk**: Exposure to demand fluctuations and competition
- **Product Liability & Warranty Risk**: Responsibility for defects and recalls
- **Inventory & Obsolescence Risk**: Risk of excess or obsolete stock
- **R&D & Technology Risk**: Innovation and technology development risk
- **Operational & Production Risk**: Production disruptions and capacity
- **Credit & Collection Risk**: Payment and receivables risk
- **Foreign Exchange Risk**: Currency exposure on inputs/outputs

## Typical transaction types

- Tangible goods (as seller)
- Services (as provider)
- Licensing of intangibles (as licensor)
